Diwali Sales Analysis

This project analyzes Diwali sales data to uncover insights into customer behavior, sales trends, and purchasing patterns across various demographics.

Overview

The Diwali Sales Analysis project aims to identify sales trends, customer preferences, and key performance metrics using data analytics techniques. The analysis covers aspects such as state-wise sales, gender-based purchases, product category performance, and more.

Key Insights

  • Higher discounts lead to increased sales, except in the Electronics category.
  • Home products generate the most revenue among all product categories.
  • Shipping fees impact sales volume, with higher fees leading to lower sales.
  • States like Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, and Karnataka contribute significantly to sales.
